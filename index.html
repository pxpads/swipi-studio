Behind the Work

How we think.
Unfiltered.

A look into the thinking, research, and craft decisions that go into every carousel we make. This is what separates good carousels from ones that actually move people.

🧪 Research Insight
We tested 12 hook styles over 3 weeks. Biggest finding: the "anti-hook" — starting mid-story — outperformed every formula-based opener by 2.3× on engagement depth.
Hook Research
⚙️ Our Workflow
Every brief goes through: idea → audience + pain → hook → structure map → slide-by-slide → review pass. Six steps. Zero filler slides. One voice: yours.
Our Process
📐 Design Philosophy
We ditched the classic 5-image grid for a hybrid text-visual rhythm: bold text → supporting visual → data point → emotional beat → CTA. When the pacing is right, people don't even realise they're reading — they're feeling. The rhythm matters more than the aesthetic. That's what we obsess over on your behalf.
Visual Design
💡 Service Insight
The CTA slide is the most under-designed part of 90% of carousels. Every carousel we deliver has a last slide that's been given the same care as the first.
Conversion
🔁 Our Rule
We write the CTA first. If we can't articulate what we want your readers to do, we don't have a carousel yet — we have notes with nowhere to go.
